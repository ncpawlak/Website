Günsquad
Android Studio/Java/XML

In a simulated shooter, the world is on the brink of peril. Command, customize, and upgrade your squad of ragtag soldiers and travel to various parts of the world, including Russia, Brazil, and the North Pole, to uncover the secrets of the beginning of WW3 and to fight to end it.

Trap of Pain
Unity/C#

Welcome to Gucci Mane's trap of pain! In this goofy shooter, you play as Gucci Mane's bodyguard and is tasked with protecting his domain from an unknown threat...zombies! Level up your character in-game, explore the humongous map, and survive as long as you can!

System Admin
Android/Java/XML

An Android app that lets the user change a variety of options, including volume sounds, alarms, and more, much like a standard Settings app on a phone.


Space Invader
Unity/C#

Space Invader is a Unity tutorial project, however it was added to by including missions and enemy waves to allow for a longer survival game.
Play as a lone space pilot who, while exploring an infamous astroid belt, discovers an advanced alien race who are not happy about you invading their territory. Hold your ground while reinforcements arrive!

Dog-Cat Neural Network
Big Red/Python/Anaconda

A final project from a Deep Learning class, this program uses Big Red's supercomputer capabilities to create a convolutional neural network (CNN) using Anaconda's deep learning library. Feeding this CNN images of dogs and cats (on the order of 1000's), it was able to learn what they look like and recognize a directory of images to be either dogs or cats; an end result of ~80% accuracy.

Command-Line Pokémon
XCode/C++

Not as exciting as the classic, command-line Pokemon is a text-based Pokemon adventure featuring the first generation of Pokemon and moves. Choose your starter and set out on am expedition into the Pokemon world...but through the command line!


MORryde Commercial
Adobe Premiere/Video production

A commercial created for MORryde during my employment that was implemented in their Facebook page, which was directed to encourage applicants to apply to their many job openings.

MORryde Help Video
Adobe Premiere/Video production

Another project created under MORryde that was used in tandem with a QR code that the customer would scan, making this video play. Using full advantage of "time whatevers" the editing features a 'puzzle piece-esque' style that encorporates animation and custom transitions.

Declaration
Adobe Photoshop

A highlighted project from school that earned a Gold Key in an annual art competition (the highest acheivement). Out of all Photoshop projects (in the Repository) this project achieved the highest honors.


FUSE Filesystem
Fusepy/Python/Raspberry Pi

Filesystem created using the Fusepy library that uses a Raspberry Pi generating random numbers via a Geiger counter, and proceeds to store them in pseudo-files for the user to view where the bits are coming from, going, and any manipulation on them.
